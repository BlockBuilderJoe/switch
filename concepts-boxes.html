FuseBoard — Card Style Alternatives

6 approaches to replacing the bordered boxes. Click nothing — just compare the feel.

A — Bordered Cards (current)

Visible border + background per fuse. Functional but boxy.

B — Recessed Cutouts

Inset shadows instead of borders. Each fuse sits in a carved slot.

C — Borderless Floating

No container at all. Ghost column dividers. Pure negative space.

D — Pill Capsules

Rounded organic shapes. Soft, approachable, less industrial.

E — DIN Rail Mount

Industrial bar behind fuses. Most authentic to real fuseboards.

F — Frosted Glass

Translucent glass cards with blur. Premium, modern feel.
