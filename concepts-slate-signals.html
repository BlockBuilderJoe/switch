Slate Minimal + Colour Signals

Same slate palette, 6 ways to bring back red/amber/green without losing the clean feel. Click to toggle.

S1 — Wires Only

Colour lives entirely in the wires + terminals.
Cards stay neutral slate.

S2 — Accent Line

Thin coloured gradient at the bottom of each card.
Wires carry colour too.

S3 — Ambient Glow

Faint coloured shadow radiates behind each card.
Atmospheric, not in-your-face.

S4 — Lever Colour

The lever itself tints red/amber/green.
Your eye goes to the lever first anyway.

S5 — Combined Subtle

Wires + lever tint + accent line. All subtle.
Multiple quiet signals reinforce each other.

S6 — LED Indicator

Tiny coloured LED dot in the top-right of each card.
Like a status light on hardware.
